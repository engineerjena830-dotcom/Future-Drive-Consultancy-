How to Apply

Apply in 5 Simple Steps

How to Pay

Payment Methods

Multiple payment methods to make it easy for students from Zimbabwe and across Africa.

Bank Transfer / EFT
Direct bank transfer to our registered accounts in China or Zimbabwe. Suitable for large amounts. Secure and traceable.
Mobile Money (EcoCash)
EcoCash and other mobile money services accepted for students in Zimbabwe. Fast and convenient for smaller payments.
Western Union / WorldRemit
International money transfer services accepted. Ideal for students across Africa without direct bank connections to China.
Cryptocurrency / USDT
We accept USDT and other major cryptocurrencies for students who prefer digital payment methods. Contact us for wallet details.

Payment Process — Step by Step

1
Submit Application & Documents
Apply through our form and submit your required documents. Our team will review and confirm eligibility.
2
Pay Application Processing Fee
Upon document approval, pay the application processing fee (varies by program). 100% refund if admission is unsuccessful.
3
Receive Acceptance Letter
After successful admission, receive your official Acceptance Letter and JW201/JW202 form for your student visa.
4
Pay Tuition to University
Tuition is paid directly to the university upon arrival in China. We guide you through the full process on campus.
